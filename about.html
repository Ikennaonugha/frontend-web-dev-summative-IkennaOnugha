About Campus Planner

Your essential tool for organizing university life.

Our Mission

Campus Planner was designed by students, for students. Our goal is to consolidate the essential tasks of university life—managing studies, tracking assignments, and maintaining focus—into a single, intuitive platform. We believe that better organization leads to less stress and better academic performance.

Core Features

  • Task Management: Easily add, categorize, and track due dates for all your assignments and responsibilities.
  • Pomodoro Timer: Utilize the integrated focus clock to apply the Pomodoro Technique, improving concentration during study sessions.
  • Calendar View: Visualize your deadlines monthly to prevent scheduling conflicts and stay ahead of the curve.
  • Responsive Design: Access your planner seamlessly on any device, from your desktop to your phone.

Technology Stack

This application is built with core web technologies to ensure speed, stability, and broad compatibility:

  • HTML5: For semantic structure and content.
  • Pure CSS: For all styling, ensuring fast loading and customization.
  • Vanilla JavaScript: For all interactive logic, including task management and the Pomodoro timer functionality.
